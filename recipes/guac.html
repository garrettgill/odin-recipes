Fajitas

Description

Guacamole is a tasty combo of all the right ingredients. Perfect for eating with chips or fajitas.

Ingredients

  • avocados
  • cilantro
  • tomatoes
  • red onion
  • jalapeños
  • salt
  • pepper
  • lime juice

Steps

  1. Cut the produce into small chunks
  2. Add the produce together (don't overdo the onions or jalapeños)
  3. Add the salt, pepper, and lime juice
  4. Mix everything together really well
  5. Taste it and adjust ingredients/spices as needed
