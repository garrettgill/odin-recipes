Smoothie

Description

Smoothies are great for a refreshing treat! Enjoy after a long workout or for a morning pick-me-up.

Ingredients

  • milk
  • yogurt
  • banana
  • strawberries
  • blueberries
  • mangoes
  • spinach
  • protein powder (optional)

    • Steps

    1. Add the fruits and vegetables to the blender
    2. Throw in two spoonfuls of yogurt
    3. Add milk, halfway up the cup
    4. Add in protein powder if included
    5. Blend and enjoy
